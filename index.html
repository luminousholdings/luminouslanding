Luminous Futures is an investment fund that exists to help enable humanity to adapt to our increasingly complex environment.

We do this by looking outward at the systems humanity lives under and by looking inward at how our minds and bodies can be adapted to optimise survival and creative capacity.

Looking outward we invest into decentralised cryptocurrency projects that support the development of resilient financial and economic systems that anyone in the world can use and build upon to help them prosper.

Looking inward we invest into longevity and psychedelic projects that can make humans more resilient and adaptable and also reduce pain and suffering.
